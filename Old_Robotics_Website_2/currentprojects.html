RoboRelay
March 2nd, 2004
Objective: Retrieve two objects at the end of playing field and bring them to the starting line.
Price Cap: Tentatively $50
    Guidelines:
  1. Only one object may be retrieved at a time
  2. Object may not be thrown to destination
  3. Object will omit IR and visible light
  4. There is a 1' by 1' length and width guideline, but no restrictions on height.
  5. Field will be 20' long with a 6'' area for the objects. There will be a 1' region for the destination of the objects.
  6. A time limit of 360 s (6 min) will be imposed.
  7. People may NOT form part of the structure of the device.

Scoring
Score = (200/T1) + (200/T2)
T1 is the time in which the robot leaves the starting area, fetches an object, and places it in the destination area.
T2 is the time from when the first object is dropped until the second object reaches the destination area.

Spring Impromptu I
January 20, 2004

Objective: Create a device using a dowel rod, a lightweight pine rod, rubber bands, poster board, staples, and paperclips to create a device to hit targets perpendicular to the floor.

Rules:

  1. The shooter: May act only to MAINTAIN potential energy in the device at the time of launch. May NOT touch projectile at time of launch.
  2. People may NOT form part of the structure of the device.
  3. People MAY stand on, or otherwise weight down, the device to keep it from moving about before/during launch so long as their presence does not help to keep the device intact (ie. so long as their presence is non-structural).
  4. Only one ball may be launched at a time.
  5. Each team gets 4 shots total.
  6. Scoring: 4 targets at increasing distance, worth 10, 20, 30, and 40 points. Targets are scored if contact is made. Knocking the CD off a target earns a +10pt bonus. Multiple targets MAY be scored by a single shot.
  7. The ball will be "dead" and cannot score further targets when it stops or exits the field of play (goes behind firing line or beyond the 40pt target). Bounces off side walls, floor, or ceiling, are still in play.
  8. The ball may also be called dead by the referee if it is moving sufficiently slowly that he doesn't want to wait for it to die by natural causes any longer.

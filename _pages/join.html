Whether you are just starting out or have years of experience, you can find a place to contribute at RAS. The best way to get involved is to join a project team. You can join a project at any point and are free to move between teams should you find one that suits you more. Check out when the current project teams are meeting below or go to the Projects Page to learn more about them. Get in touch by going below and signing up on our email list and joining us on slack. Find us everywhere else by going to our Contact Page.




Subscribe to our mailing list to receive updates from RAS:



Slack

Join us on Slack, a powerful messanging service where we coordinate project teams, general announcments, ask for help, and discuss anything we want. Sign up with your Pitt email at Slack. Make a post in #general and introduce yourself!


Email

Email ras@pitt.edu if you have any questions or want to get involved.

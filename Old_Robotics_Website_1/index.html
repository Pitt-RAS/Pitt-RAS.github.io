The Robotics Club is an organization that promotes experimentation with robotics, provides a forum for discussion and sharing information, and gives its members a community to participate in and learn about the field of robotics.

Meetings are held on Tuesday evenings, at 7:30 p.m.,
in our lab, room 466 Benedum Hall,
(if we're not there, check room 420)
unless otherwise noted.

In Our Site...

Prospective members: click here to tell us about yourself.

In the Member's Area you'll find project proposal forms.

The Projects section contains information on current, as well as past, undertakings by our members. This includes: robots, computer projects, competition results, written papers, etc.

Check the Calender for upcoming events and meetings.

The Image Gallery holds pictures of various events/robots we've come across.

News includes articles of interest about robotics and important happenings in the club.

Browse our extensive Links section for more general robotics information.

Click on Contact to find out who we are and how to reach us.

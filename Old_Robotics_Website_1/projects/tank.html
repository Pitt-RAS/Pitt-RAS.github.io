Project Leader: Angela A. DeFrancesco

Description: This project got started when I was taking apart an old printer and found the paper feeders. Right now I am just trying to put together a simple circuit to make the thing avoid obstacles. Since I have since found more treads from more printers, I am hoping to later make a little "army" of these things, programmed with PICs or stamps.

Pitt Robotics Club
Line Following, Spring 2009

Rules

Robots must follow a black line over a whitish surface (the floor of Benedum Hall). Fastest robot to the end wins. There is a time penalty whenever the robot leaves the line.

Outcome

5 groups entered their robots. Every robot except one finished the course without ever leaving the line, which was a great accomplishment compared to our last line follower competition. We are working hard to build on our success.

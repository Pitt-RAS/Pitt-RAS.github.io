Pitt Robotics Club
Racing Sumo Bots, 2009-2010
Sumo robots are burly and slow, right? Wrong! This competition presented a dual challenge that required groups to create a robot that could compete in a sumo-wrestling like event and also race against the other robots. The robot had to do both without any human-completed hardware changes. Software changes were allowed between the race and sumo events. The idea was to take a previous competition and scale it up; see the earlier competition here. The competition was held in April 2010. Pictures the competition entries are coming soon!

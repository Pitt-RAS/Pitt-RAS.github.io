Current Competition: Pirate Adventure
1. Set sail and locate an island and land on it. (Land being defined as completely removed from the water and sitting still on the sand (or whatever material). The island can be detected with microphone that detects a noise from the island, sonar, lasers, whatever means necessary.
2. Locate treasure (on a new map) and dig it up!

This is just a rough idea of what the competition will be like. The rules and ideas will be decided before the first meeting
